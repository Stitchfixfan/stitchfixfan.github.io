Welcome to the Stitch Fix Buy/Sell/Trade Website!

Welcome to the website for people who love the Stitch Fix Facebook groups and have the following goals:

    1. You want a more efficient way to look for clothing for sale in your size.
    2. You want to maximize the number of potential buyers of your item, which this site does by allowing people to search by size.
    3. You have something from stitch fix you love but no longer fits and want to see if you can trade with someone for another size.
    4. You like getting clothes for free and are willing to trade some of your no longer worn items for others and want to get trade offers.

Please click on this link to create an account and start building your dream wardrobe.
